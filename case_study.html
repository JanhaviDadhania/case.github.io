Guidlines

• Competition is exclusively for the students and research scholars of IIT Dharwad

• Participation should be in teams consisting a minimum of 2 students and a maximum of 3 students

• There is no restriction on the branches or batches to form a team.

• Students are supposed to register their teams through the Google Form rolled out by the organisers

• However, in cases where a student cannot form a team, they could register themselves individually through the Google Form and the organisers will assign them to a team, which will be binding on them

• There will be two rounds in the competition

• Round 1 (Prelims): 8PM on 19.02.21 to 5PM on 20.02.21

- Submission of report on the case as per the guidelines issued by the organisers

• Round 2 (Final): 2PM to 5PM on 21.02.21

- 6 teams will be shortlisted for Round 2 - Shortlisted teams are required to give a 10 minute presentation before the jury, followed by 10 minutes of Q&A, on Google Meet

• Top two teams will be awarded with certificates, attractive prizes and inducted into the IIC Hall of Fame

• All participants will be issued certificates of participation
